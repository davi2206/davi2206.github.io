The Decree

1. Use common sense

This server is a safe and fun place for everyone. If you are not contributing to that, you are doing something wrong

2. Do as you are told, when you are told

If someone asks you to change your behavior, respect them, and do as instructed.
No one will be giving out orders, but if someone feels the need to comment on your behavior or actions, you might be doing something wrong.
See rule #1

3. No second chances

If you fail to comply with rules #1 and #2, you are not a good match for our community, and we suggest you find another server to play on. We will not be responding nor reacting to requests to lift a ban

Domains

There are several different areas to explore on the server. Some you may know, and some are specific to NorthLore. Each has it's own charm.

World

The regular overworld, where you can live, build farm, mine and create communities. Here you can have a home, and play the game as it was intended

Protection stones are available to claim land with /kits

Creative

For all your creative and artistic needs and projects. You cannot claim land here, but everyone are expected to respect eachothers creations in all the domains

The Nether

When the World gets too boring, you can go to the Nether to have a challenge, or look for treasure, that is not available in the overworld

Protection stones can be used here, if you want a less hospitable home

Adventures

A world of adventure challenges. Here are mazes, parkour, drop-maps.. And such

At least thats the plan. Not much is built there yet. Please remind me to update the world and this description if I forget

The End

No one has ever gone here, so we don't know for sure what is there. Rumours say dragons, but that seems unlikely, even for us

You cannot use protection stones here. Nothing is protected here

Experimental

This is where we started, when we were setting up things. We have kept the world as a place where we can test everything from railways to TNT contraptions

You cannot claim land here, and nothing is safe, as The Scribs may blow up the world, if he gets bored

Groups

Stray

You start here. You have some permissions, and can claim land, but you are somewhat limited. Start your adventure, and you will soon become a Settler

Settler

The most common tier. Established members of the community. You can claim more land and access more places

Pioneer

A select group of trusted people. You can have an estate and go almost whereever you want. They will also been granted some cosmetic perks, such as fancy hats

Guard

They are here to protect the fun and the peace. If you need anythin, they are here to help. Feel free to ask

The Scribe

The All-Powerfull few. Only MasterAlerion has this title, as it grants all the permission in the universe, and should only be used for development, and trolling Strays

Communications

All communications is handled through Discord. If you are not a member there, you probably arent supposed to try and access the server. If you think you should have access, contact someone on the server directely, and we can talk about your options.
