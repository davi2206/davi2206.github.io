First Travels

In the early summer of 2025 I decided to finally get out of my comfortzone and go traveling.
I booked flights and hotel on a small island in Greece, and hoped for the best.
This is the legend of my journey.

May 25th

I want to travel, and have been talking about it for ages. But I am too scared to do anything about it. Or so I have been telling myself, at least. But the fear has to end. I have to stop sabotagig myself at every corner.
I started looking at trips adn found something interesting, through the senior sosciety I am supporting.
I had my reservations about taking a trip with seniors, but it looked interesting. Flights, hotel and trips on the island, all booked in one easy sweap.
I decided to go for it. Can't be worse than sitting at home, doing fuck all.

May 26th

The travel agency called. They wanted to make sure I was aware that the trip is meant for seniors (50+), so they were wondering if it is the right choice for me, and they offered to refund me, if I changed my mind.
I have been thinking about it most of the day, and maybe it isn't the right choice for me? It would be pretty easy to call and get my money back
Many considerations and looking at alternative travels later, I have decided I need to go. Since I didn't find a better trip to book, I am sticking with what I already ordered. I'm going to Greece, and I'm going to get teh best possible trip out of it.
